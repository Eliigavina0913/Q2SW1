Name Characters Description
Lincold Loud Lincoln Loud is the main character of the Nickelodeon cartoon series The Loud House. As the middle child (and only boy) in the Loud family, Lincoln has ten sisters and is a fan of manga, comics, and video games.
Luan Loud Luan Loud is the fourth child of the Loud family. Luan is a goofy and fun-loving girl who is the clown of the family. She loves to crack jokes and play pranks, and never is seen without a whoopee cushion or squirt flower on her.
Lana Loud Lana Loud is a character in the Nickelodeon show, The Loud House. At seven years old, she is the fourth youngest of the Loud siblings, as well as Lola's (older by two minutes) twin sister. Both attend second grade, but that's where their similarities end. In contrast to Lola's girly personality, Lana is a tomboy. She enjoys the outdoors, namely playing in the mud and finding frogs. She is an expert junior plumber, and no job is too gross or tough to handle.
